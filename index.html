Practical tools

Small things that actually help.

01

Name what you feel

Putting a name on your emotion — "I feel anxious" — immediately reduces its power over you.

02

Unfollow to breathe

It's okay to unfollow accounts that make you feel small. Curate your feed like it's your mental health.

03

Talk to someone

A friend, a counselor, a parent — saying things out loud takes weight off your chest.

04

Rest without guilt

Rest is productive. Your brain and body need downtime to function. Resting is not laziness.

05

Set your own bar

Define success on your own terms, not the world's. What does "doing well" mean to you?

06

Celebrate small wins

Got out of bed? Finished something small? That counts. Progress is progress, no matter the size.

You are more than your productivity.
